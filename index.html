The Journey

Where I Started

My career began in the world of structure and systems—product management, consulting, entrepreneurship, and software development. I built applications, designed strategies, and solved complex business problems. Everything had a framework, a process, a clear objective.

I was good at it. But something was missing.

The Questions

The questions started small: What if technology could tell stories? What if code could be expressive, not just functional? What if systems could surprise you?

I found myself drawn to the edges—where logic met creativity, where data became visual, where interaction became experience. The clean lines of my career path started to blur.

The Transformation

I dove into digital media and creative coding. p5.js became my canvas. WebXR opened new dimensions. Interactive storytelling is merging everything I'd learned about structure with everything I'm discovering about expression.

The shift wasn't abandoning my technical foundation—it was building on top of it. I'm merging everything I'd learned about structure with everything I'm discovering about expression.

Where I Am Now

Today, I'm at the intersection of technology and creativity—bringing my technical foundation into new territory, designing with both logic and creative expression.

I'm building interactive experiences and exploring what happens when technology becomes a tool for creative expression—not just functional solutions.
